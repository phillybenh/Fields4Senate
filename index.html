MEET LINDA

Linda Fields has lived her life with a defining purpose: to serve others and to fight for what is right. She worked for 28 years as a National Organizer & Business Agent for the National Union of Hospital and Healthcare Employees, District 1199C, AFSCME, AFL-CIO until 2018. She has successfully obtained higher and fairer wages, employee benefits and workers’ rights for hospital workers for some of the largest hospital chains in Pennsylvania. She currently works as a Special Assistant for Laborers’ International Union of North America Local 135 in Norristown, PA.

She is currently running in the 2020 Democratic Primary for the Pennsylvania State Senate in the 17th District in Montgomery and Delaware Counties.
